TicketPRO (event ticketing system with QR codes)

My final project at the Developers Institute developed during 2.5 weeks. It is user-friendly online event ticketing system, built on Node.js, React.js, JWT, AWS and PostgreSQL that aims to simplify the process of event organization and ticket purchasing.Project provides users with a seamless and convenient way to discover, browse, and book tickets for various events such as concerts, sports events, conferences, and more. Additionally, the system includes QR codes on tickets for enhanced security and ease of entry to events.

Winner and got the special award "Best overall project"
